Overview: How Multiple Websites & Stores Work

  • Global: the entire installation.
  • Websites: "parents" of stores, can share customer data or not.
  • Stores (or store view groups): "children" of websites, in which Products and Categories are managed. A root category is configured for each store view group, so, which allows multiple stores under the same website to have totally different catalog structures.
  • Store View:: A store needs one or more store views to be browse-able in the front-end. The catalog structure per store view will always be the same, it simply allows for multiple presentations of the data in the front. 90% of implementations will likely use store views to allow customer to switch between 2 or more languages.
